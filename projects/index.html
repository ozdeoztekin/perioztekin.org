A.R.A.I.A.

An AI app for aphasia recovery

For people who’ve had a stroke and can’t find their words anymore, but who are still completely there. We built the therapy around singing: when language won’t come, the music sometimes still does. Two weeks at Stanford CNI-X 2025, a six-person team, my capstone.

NeuroCalm

A wearable for sensory regulation

A headband I’m designing to read alpha and theta brainwaves and give a calming cue before sensory overload tips into crisis. Built around consumer EEG and an Arduino, with a prototype I can run on simulated input while iterating.
